GREEN CONSTRUCTION SIMULATION ANALYSIS

绿色建筑模拟分析

SOFTWARE INTRODUCTION

软件简介

Green construction simulation analysis software (hereinafter referred to as IES) is developed by British IES company integrated green building performance simulation analysis software

THE MAIN FUNCTION

主要功能

THE MAIN FUNCTION

主要功能特点

(1) to establish a three-dimensional informatization construction model, or import other modeling tools to establish model, such as Revit or Sketchup, etc.
(2) ApacheLoad according to ASHRAE (American society of heating, refrigeration and air conditioning engineering is) standard for cold and hot load calculation.
(3) building annual energy consumption, CO2 emissions and indoor air quality and calculate the annual energy consumption of air conditioning system.
(4) simulation building natural ventilation rate, as a result, used for calculation of energy consumption.
(5) computing architecture, both inside and outside of natural lighting, can be calculated results for the visual effect.
(6) building inside and outside of natural lighting and artificial lighting, can be calculated results for the visual effect
(7) calculate building block coefficient, the sun and shade coefficient can be used in thermal simulation, can dynamically display changes of sunlight throughout the day.
(8) in the form of dynamic simulation calculation within the building all the time required to leave the building.
(9) by finite volume method, calculating building 3 d wind environment, the simulation of the indoor temperature field, flow field, etc.; Through the post-processing, output speed and temperature contours, such as thermal comfort calculation results.
